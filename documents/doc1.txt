study up on all animals, perhaps there's a youtube video that gives a description for all of them, mainly cattle, horses/donkeys, tamable mobs, dolphins (give a speed boost), how to breed cattle / with what

for combat

don't spam your attack like a madman. There's an indicator that's fullness determines how strong the attack will be so it's best to attack just before it's empty

while falling, which usually happens after jumping, you critical strike

use a shield. Shields are awesome. Also, you need to hold out your shield a bit early or it won't work, but you can also stop using it a bit early.

not sure if this is optimal but when I fight a skeleton I approach it while sprinting in a spiral, then hit it with a crit, block an arrow with a shield, before it fires as I hold my shield, I jump again, block it midair and critical strike

I guess you should jump before the indicator is full since you'll deal most damage while falling thus it'll take a bit of time until that happens

you can craft golden apples that'll give you a huge defensive boost, though it'll cost you 8 gold so use them only in emergencies. Can also be found in treasure chests like in temples. Oh, and if you find a temple and descend to the floor below it, don't step on the pressure palte. You can mine it, then mine below and loot the TNT as well

food restores hunger and saturation

here's the wiki, scroll down to the board with all foods

1 value is for hunger, the one you can see, the other is for saturation- invisible of course. While hunger can be restored until a limit stockpiling from each food eaten, saturation does not stack. It is used up as a substitute for hunger while there's any left

hunger is used for physical activity and for healing. If you have full or nearly full hunger, you'll regenerate, but if you have a ton of saturation while having full/nearly full hunger, you'll regenerate extremely fast, thus high-saturation foods work like healing items thus eating something like meat will be far better than a watermelon slice for healing, even if your hunger is only 1 chicken nugget away from being full

as you'll see in that chart, the king of food items is a certain mushroom soup. You can craft mushroom soup from a bowl and 2 different (non-nether-exclusive) mushrooms, but if you add a flower in the process it'll also give you a short buff. Adding a yellow small flower or a light blue one will give you a saturation buff thus that soup's total value is the greatest
